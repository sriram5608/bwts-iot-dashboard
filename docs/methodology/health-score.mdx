## What the health score is

The system health score is a **0â€“100 composite metric** stored in the `bwts_iot_health_scores` table. It represents the overall condition of the BWTS at each telemetry timestamp. The score is displayed as the arc gauge on the Overview tab's top-right corner and as a trend line in the Trend Analysis tab.

| Component | Field | What it measures |
|-----------|-------|-----------------|
| UV Health | `uv_health` | How closely `UVR_INTENSITY` matches the target operating point (650 W/mÂ˛) |
| Lamp Health | `lamp_health` | Aggregate efficiency of all 16 lamps relative to their rated output |
